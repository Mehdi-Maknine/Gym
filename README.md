# Gym Management for Odoo

This Odoo module provides a complete gym management solution, including:

## ✅ Features

### Members & Plans
- Register and manage gym members
- Assign membership plans with pricing and duration
- Track trainer assignments

### Scheduling & Attendance
- Schedule class sessions with max capacity
- Assign trainers to classes
- Member enrollment and attendance tracking
- “Mark All Present” button for quick check-in

### Membership Management
- Expiry tracking and visual alerts
- Automated email reminders before expiration

### Payments
- Record payments by member and plan
- Payment history in member profiles
- Total revenue tracking

### Dashboards & Reporting
- Admin dashboard with KPIs (active members, revenue, etc.)
- Calendar view of sessions
